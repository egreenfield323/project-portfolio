Running the livestream at church.

I've been volunteering in tech at my Church since I was very young.

During COVID, my dad and I ran all the services remotely from home.

As we transitioned back in person, I designed a more substantial stream/audio system.

Before leaving for college, I trained other youth in live streaming basics, and they now run the stream every Sunday.
